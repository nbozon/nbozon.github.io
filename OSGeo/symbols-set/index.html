    About

    This is the OSGeo symbol set, a visual representation of the Open Source Geospatial Foundation projects and activities.

    The 48 symbols are derivated from the projects logotypes and the OSGeo foundation brand assets. Most of them have been simplified, reworked or completely redrawn in order to ensure consistency and scalability of the symbols set.

    The OSGeo symbols set composed of two parts, both copyrighted OSGeo:

    • - The SVG source files, available under the CC-BY-SA license.
    •  
    • - A cross-browser web font, available under the OFL license

    This work is not exhaustive and subject to change!
    Please review and comment it, or bring new ideas on the dedicated pull request.
